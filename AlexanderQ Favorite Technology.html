However, over time, games like DOOM and increases in fps allowed games to feel 3d and gain the feeling it has today. ![w:500 , ](https://www.gamespot.com/a/uploads/original/313/3136061/3649446-doooom.jpg) ---- # Types of Fps games Type | Multiplayer | Brief description --- | --- | --- Tactical | Yes | More focus on positioning Movement | Yes | Games that often have heavy movement systems RPG | No | Game with a focus on story MilSim | Mostly | Games with a focus on realism Survival | Mostly | Games with a focus on survival and upgrades. --- # Tactical shooters Tactical shooter are often recognized by their slow movement speed and value on positioning and tactics. Notable examples include CSGO and Valorant, and Rainbow Six siege. ![w:350 , h:250](https://media.steampowered.com/apps/csgo/blog/images/fb_image.png?v=6) ![w:300 , h:200](https://encrypted-tbn0.gstatic.com/images?q=tbn:ANd9GcTAYqC0O0PHeriZcK3e2o5OM0oduQT9exq8wdE_G6VbtOl7Oq-PwsXpyKshN6wTjF4M0CE&usqp=CAU) ![w:300 , h:300](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/4/47/Tom_Clancy%27s_Rainbow_Six_Siege_cover_art.jpg) --- # Movement These games often have very fast movement mechanics and fast paced gameplay, with many modes such as deathmatch or CTF. Your stereotypical FPS game. Examples include the COD multiplayer. ![bg right](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/YuAO1LK1K14/maxresdefault.jpg) --- # RPG RPG games often don't have multiplayer and have the player fight NPCs instead, often in story based maps. Examples include most campaigns in games like Halo or COD. ![bg left](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/WvdAPR0TwQs/maxresdefault.jpg) --- # Milsim These games have a heavy emphasis on realism, with things like almost instant kills. bullet drop and more realistic magazine counts. Examples include ARMA and Ready or Not. ![bg right](https://cdn.akamai.steamstatic.com/steam/apps/1144200/ss_810cdf4f23943a805d5999ba5556c1078f017b1a.1920x1080.jpg?t=1705678438) --- # Survival These games have the play face enemies, most usually in a wave system. The player gets better upgrades as the game goes on. Examples include COD zombies. ![bg left](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/BhjRCiayLVY/maxresdefault.jpg) --- # Why I like it One of the things I most enjoy is the fast paced gameplay many of these games have. They are very intense and are extremely fun. No matter your skill level, it is fun to play these games.
